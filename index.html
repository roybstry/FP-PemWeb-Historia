? Why Historia

  • Everyone’s here

    Historia is active in 150 countries and 75 languages ​​and its users are diverse and of all ages. It is also the video application installed on the most smartphones in the world

  • Creative expression

    Creativity and self-expression are the driving fuel of our community and a large part of them are active content producers and not just passive viewers. Our creators will be happy to create authentic, creative and diverse content together with your brands

  • Cultural Impact

    What starts on Historia, doesn't just stay on Historia. The content that people create reaches all the major platforms and gets wide exposure. As a result Historia has the power to start trends and influence the mainstream

  • Content first

    The famous cliché "content is king" gets a whole new expression! Their brand does not need to chase followers and likes - if your content is good and interesting, it will reach a wide exposure and levels of involvement that you did not know

  • Sound on

    In Historia, the music is a significant element, which also makes it the First screen. Most of our users don't scroll while watching another screen and in a world where attention is the hardest thing to achieve this is a huge advantage

  • A full screen experiance

    Historia's vertical format allows your videos to play in full screen, without competition or distractions. And we already talked about the attention war right
